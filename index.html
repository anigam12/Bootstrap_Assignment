While they arrive at our doorstep due to a variety of circumstances – surrendered, abandoned, homeless or neglected – each one deserves a loving home. The Thrift Store

  • Opportunities
  • Location & Hours

Just like the shelter, our Thrift Store needs plenty of volunteers to help keep its doors open. All time donated at the Thrift Store benefits the cats and dogs at the Animal Shelter.

Thrift Store volunteers must be at least 17 years old. A few simple steps to follow and you’re on your way to being a volunteer:

Print out and complete a Volunteer Application, or pick one up at the store.

Set up an appointment with Thrift Store Manager-Donna Smith for a tour and introduction to the store setup and needs.

Sign up on the schedule. Begin volunteering!


The Thrift Store is located - downtown at 301 Lenora St., McCall, ID.

Store Hours for shopping and donation drop-off:

WINTER HOURS: Open 10-5 Mon-Sat, and closed on Sunday
